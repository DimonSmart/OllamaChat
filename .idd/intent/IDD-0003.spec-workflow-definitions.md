# IDD-0003.spec-workflow-definitions

## Intent

Provide a catalog for creating, validating, editing, restoring, and deleting programmable agent workflows.

## Related Specifications

Related: IDD-0002, IDD-0005.

## Behavior

Users can search saved definitions, inspect their identity and kind, edit workflow source, validate it before saving, and restore shipped definitions without deleting custom definitions.

## Durable Architecture And Constraints

A saved workflow derives its visible identity, description, and orchestration kind from valid source.

## Non-Goals

This surface does not execute a workflow; execution belongs to agent orchestrations and chat.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Invalid workflow source cannot be saved as a runnable definition.
- Saved definitions are searchable and editable.
- Restoring built-ins preserves custom definitions.

## Verification

Automated coverage verifies compilation validation, persistence, deletion, and built-in restoration semantics.
